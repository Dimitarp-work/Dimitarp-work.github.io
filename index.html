Hello I am Dimitar and this is my Website

If you are here and are reading this, that means you want to know why I chose ICT. While it's a rather simple answer to be honest, after all, I have loved computers from a hardware and software perspective in my whole life. Now how it all began, I will tell the story with this website.

Now it all began with me studing lots and lots of math and getting more bored with every next day, but then I was introduced to programming and started my first course for programming at the young age of 13. But after 2 long years, I decided that I would rather prefer the hardware aspect for the time being and go to a networking high school, where my interest became even bigger.

Now let me get this clear—I have been disassembling electronics since I was 4, so I think I have always been interested in computers in one way or another. This passion has carried through my life as I worked as a computer technician when I was 16 and 17.
